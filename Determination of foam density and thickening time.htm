1.    PURPOSE AND SCOPE

Determination of density and thickening time of foam.

2.    PROCESS DESCRIPTION

 

2.1 Occupational safety.

 

Safety ruled have to be followed at incoming inspection laboratory. The test is allowed to be performed only by person familiar with the working instruction of the Tyoe EWTM industrial mixer, Metabo band saw and signed on the instruction familiarization sheet.

 

2.2  Required equipment and chemicals

 

o   industrial mixer

o   Immersion thermometer Testo 925

o   mixer attachments

o   foam box with plastic bag

o   Polyol (#8001090833)

o   isocyanate (MDI) (#9000546643)

o   C-pentane (#9000117001)

o   paper cups (250ml)

o   gloves

o   wooden stick

o   Protective glasses

o   scales

o   flask with distilled water

o   plastic container 500 ml

 

 

Scales Sartorius Band saw Metabo Mixer, type EWTM

 

2.3  Test execution

 

1.     Determine the foam thickening time (preparation of foam components for mixing)

Place an empty plastic container without a lid with a narrow metal stirrer on the scales, reset the weight.

2.           Pour exactly 300 g of polyol into this container, reset the weight

4. Add 43,4 g of c-Pentan

5. Mix the compounds at mixer at 1500 rpm during 30 seconds

6. You should perform this operation until the weight of c-pentan is equal 42,6 g.


 

7. This mixture of poliol and c-pentan with isocyanate (MDI) place to thermobath for 30 minutes at 25 degrees.

 

8. After 30 minutes, measure the temperature of the MDI and the Polyol/pentane mixture using a Testo 925 immersion thermometer. The temperature of the components should be 25.0 0.3 C.

 

 

If the temperature is not within the specified limits, it is necessary to place the materials again in a thermal bath for 30 minutes.

2. Determine the foam thickening time (foam preparation)

 

Take a paper cup, put it on the scales - set it to zero, pour exactly 114.2 g of a mixture of polyol and c-pentane into it - set it to zero.

 

 

Then add to a paper cup exactly 152 g, MDI (Add it fastly, because the reaction is starting)

 

 

Then mix the components with a mixer during 5 seconds. At the same time, turn on the stopwatch and turn on the mixer. Then without turning off the stopwatch, pour the mixture into the box.

 

 

Wait 35 seconds, after which we begin to pierce the foam with a wooden stick until the fibers begin to appear, as soon as the fibers appear, we stop the stopwatch. This time is the foam thickening time.

 

3. Determine the density of the foam.

 

After 20-25 minutes after mixing from the center of the resulted foam, we cut out 3 cubes with a length of 5 cm on each side.

 

We weigh one cube and write down its mass (m), then immerse the cube with a wooden stick in the center of the flask with distilled water. We write down the results of the scales, at the moment when the cube is completely immersed in water (V).
